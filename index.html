Bar and Restaurant:


Located on the ground floor of Dorm Wing, open from 6:30 AM to 12:00 AM (last food order at 10:30 PM).

We serve a variety of Asian and Western dishes, refreshing beverages, flavorful coffee, freshly baked treats, and a variety of alcoholic drinks available at the bar.

Transportation

In Cambodia, you can use ride-hailing apps like Grab and PassApp to get around the city. You can also ask tuk-tuks directly on the street for a ride.

Note: These apps may not work in some areas of the country.



If you're looking for a bus or ferry ticket, we can help you book it! Please visit the reception.

Check-Out Information

• Check-out time is before 12:00 PM.

• For late check-out requests, please inform reception in advance. Approval depends on room availability.

• Late check-out until 4:00 PM: 50% of the nightly rate

• After 4:00 PM: Full nightly rate will be charged

• Guests can store luggage after check-out (same day only).

• For valuables, please use the small lockers at reception or keep them with you.

• After check-out, you’re still welcome to use our facilities for the rest of the day.
